Purpose of Anash

The purpose of Anash is to promote and develop community of Anash Chabad Lubavitch. It will work to promote unity amongst Anash, publicize and promote the directions of the Lubavitcher Rebbe, establish elected Anash Vaad Rabbonim for halachic guidance, and to provide assistance to Anash families.

Membership

Jewish according to Halacha. Resident in the state of Victoria. Conducts oneself in accordance with the standard specified in the description of Anash. Age 25 years or older for membership. Application subsequently to be approved by special committee.

Suggested membership fee $36 per year, minimum $5.

The standard of “Anash” required for membership is as a man who: studies Chabad maamorim and sichos as published by the Lubavitch movement; associates with other Anash in their studies and farbregens; fulfills the Chabad custom of chitas, rambam, davens nusach Chabad and conducts themselves according to Chabad custom.
